## Related Tax Information

### **Overview of Missouri Taxes**

Missouri’s **state income tax** is **progressive**, with tax rates ranging from **1.5% to 5.4%** based on your income. In addition to state taxes, you will also have **federal income tax** and **Social Security** deductions taken out of your paycheck. Here are the key taxes to consider when calculating your take-home pay:

- **Missouri State Income Tax**: Missouri’s **state income tax** is progressive, ranging from **1.5% to 5.4%**, depending on your income level.
- **Federal Income Tax**: This tax is calculated based on your income and filing status, using the IRS tax brackets.
- **Social Security Tax**: The rate is **6.2%** on wages up to $160,200 (for 2025).
- **Medicare Tax**: The rate is **1.45%**, with an additional **0.9%** surtax for income over $200,000 (for single filers).
- **Other Deductions**: Your employer may also deduct other items such as health insurance premiums, retirement contributions, or other employee benefits.

|--------------------------|--------------|

### **How Missouri’s Progressive Tax System Works**

Missouri’s state income tax is progressive, meaning that the more you earn, the higher the tax rate on your income. For example, the first $1,053 of your income is taxed at **1.5%**, while income over $7,370 is taxed at **5.4%**.
